Full description:

Tenure: Leasehold

Arguably one of the best located apartments on the development, being on the extreme outskirts of Fairfield Hall featuring its own terrace and lovely views over lawns and mature trees to two sides. This outstanding two bedroom ground floor property is set within East Wing which looks towards the cricket pitch to the front of the Hall, beyond the expansive lawns in a southerly direction. We would suggest accessing the flat from Door 5 which is at the rear (Pryor Wing) as this provides more convenient access.

Forming part of a skilful conversion by P.J. Livesey. Fairfield Hall is a landmark building, converted with sympathy to reflect its Victorin origins whilst also creating contemporary apartments with practical, well specified accommodation. Originally dating from the mid-1850s the impressive former hospital building is set within glorious mature grounds that include a cricket pitch and many notable trees, together with interesting walks, cycle ways and gated vehicular access.

This particular two double bedroom ground floor apartment provides elegant, well proportioned accommodation that features a particularly light and airy reception room with access to it s own terrace with a southerly aspect. Overall, a lovely spacious room for entertaining, incorporating an open-plan kitchen area. The master bedroom features an en-suite shower room, whilst bedroom two is a dual aspect double bedroom. There is a family bathroom together with the practical benefit of gas fired central heating (not tested). There is a parking space allocated to the apartment, which is set back, facing landscaped grounds and enjoying all the facilities of Fairfield Park; which also includes a school, on-site GL14 gym and pool and a Tesco Express store.

COMMUNAL GARDENS:
Fairfield Hall is set in acres of parkland, including formal lawns and informally landcaped gardens. Traditional 'village' cricket pitch, walkways and wooded areas, all providing interest and creating an attractive environment in which to live. Gravelled parking area approached via long driveway with electrically operated cast-iron gates providing access. Many mature trees plus shrubbery, hedges and appropriate planting all help to maintain the quiet atmosphere and surroundings. This particular apartment is at the far eastern end of the site, with open space to the front and side enjoying privacy and a quiet setting.

Tenure: Leasehold

An amazing buy! A luxurious well appointed 2 bedroom apartment in prestigious Fairfield Hall situated within 100 s of acres of stunning parkland with facilities including GL-14 Health Club & Doughty s Brasserie. Open House viewings between 10am - 11am every Saturday, closing date 12 noon on 9 February 2011.

Ground Floor

Communal Entrance
Via communal entrance with access into apartment.

First Floor

Entrance
Via hardwood door, leading into:

Entrance Hall
Coving to smooth skimmed ceiling. Double radiator. White panel doors to understairs storage cupboard and door to lounge. Dog leg staircase leading to first floor.

Lounge/Dining Room
19' 7" x 14' (5.97m x 4.27m)
Coving to smooth skimmed ceiling. Two Georgian Bar windows to rear aspect. Two double radiators. Opening to kitchen area.

Kitchen
8' 5" x 7' 3" (2.57m x 2.21m)
Smooth skimmed ceiling. Extractor fan. Fitted with a range of eye and base level units providing storage with rolledged worksurfaces and complementary tiled splashbacks. Laminate flooring. Inset sink with drainer and mixer tap. Inset four ring gas hob with extractor incorporating light over and oven under. Integrated dishwasher and washer/dryer.

Second Floor

Landing
Velux window. Stairs from entrance hall. White panel door to airing cupboard housing Potterton boiler. Smooth skimmed ceiling with coving. Doors to all rooms.

Bedroom 1
12' 10" x 13' 4" (3.91m x 4.06m)
Smooth skimmed ceiling with coving. Velux window to front. Exposed beam. Double radiator. Telephone and TV points. White panel door to:

En-Suite Shower Room
Smooth skimmed ceiling with extractor fan. Velux window to rear. Part tiled walls. Suite comprising single shower cubicle, pedestal mounted wash hand basin and low level wc. Laminate flooring. Double radiator.

Bedroom 2
9' 5" x 11' 2" (2.87m x 3.40m)
Velux window to front. Smooth skimmed ceiling. Exposed beam. Double radiator. Telephone and TV points.

Bathroom
Smooth skimmed ceiling with extractor fan. Part tiled walls. Laminate flooring. Double radiator. Suite comprising panel enclosed bath with mixer tap/shower attachment, pedestal mounted wash hand basin and low level wc.

Outside

Communal Grounds & Parking
Parking space plus additional visitors parking.


FAIRFIELD PARK with its breathtaking grounds and mature woodland provide an excellent range of facilities including GL-14 Health Club with Doughty’s Brasserie and a well regarded lower school. Situated on the Herts/Beds border just five minutes from junction 10 of the A1(M). There is a mini bus that runs a shuttle service from the gates of Fairfield Park to Arlesey train station, with a direct link to Kings Cross (journey time 35 minutes approx), alternatively Letchworth train station provides a direct link to Kings Cross in approx 25 mins.
